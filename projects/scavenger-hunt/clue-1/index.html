How To Play

Welcome to play our supercool HOT&COLD scavenger hunt game.

It works best on smartphone, go down and listen to our clue, which is a sound. Also, remember to turn on the location service on your phone. You can find it your settings. If you are so cool that you have 4G, use that - or just go for 3G.

After you have listened to the sound, go down to the map slide and have fun. The color will change compared how near you are to the goal. After you think you have found the correct place. Take a picture, go on twitter and post the image with a hashtag.
