Hot & Cold

Welcome to our scavenger hunt. To begin, use a smartphone with location services turned on and 3G (4G if you're super cool). Also, make sure you are disconnected from Wi-Fi.

How to play

Read the clues below and listen to the sound to help you identify where it came from.

The red dot on the map shows your current location. A prompt above the map will change according to how near you are to the destination. The closer you are, the warmer the colour of the background and the further away you are, the cooler the colour. If the background colour changes to red, you are within 5 meters of the sound's location.

When you think you have found where the sound came from, take a photo of it and tweet it with #ravehotncold.

Clues

"I move but I stay in the same place."

"Thousands of people walk past me a day but only a few use me."

"Im open to all."
