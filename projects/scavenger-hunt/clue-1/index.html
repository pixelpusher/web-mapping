Welcome to our Hot&Cold scavenger hunt. Use a smartphone with location services turned on, and 3G (4G if you're super cool). Also, make sure you are disconnected from Wi-Fi.

How to play

Scroll down to the sound, listen to and read the clues then slide to the map and have fun. The red dot shows your location. The background colour and a prompt above the map will change according to how near you are to the goal.

The closer you are the warmer the colour and the further away you are, the cooler the colour. Once the background colour has changed to red you are with in 5 meters of the sounds location.

When you think you have found the sounds location. Take a picture and tweet the image with a hashtag #ravehotncold.

Clues

"I move but I stay in the same place."

"Thousands of people walk past me a day but only a few use me."

"Im open to all."
