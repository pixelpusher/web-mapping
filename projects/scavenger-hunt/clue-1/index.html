How To Play

Welcome to our supercool HOT&COLD geolocation online scavenger hunt. It works best on smartphones, make sure to turn on the location service (which can be found in your phones settings).

If you are super cool and have 4G, use that - if not 3G works equally well. Make sure you are disconnected from Wi-Fi.

Next step

Scroll down to the sound, listen to and read the clues then slide to the map and have fun. The red dot tells you . The background color and a prompt above the map will change according to how near you are to the goal. The closer you are the warmer the colour, the further away the cooler the colour. Once the background colour has changed to red you are with in 5 meters of the sounds location. When you think you have found the sounds location.
